## GoalWorld — Executive Summary

**GoalWorld** is the world's first SportsFi Protocol on Solana, designed to capture the 5 billion fan base of the 2026 FIFA World Cup. We transform passive football spectators into Professional Player Agents and Sports Traders using blockchain technology.

**Product:** Our platform features 528 real football players minted as high-fidelity NFTs (Genesis Squad), powered by the Infinity Engine — a "Zero Value Loss" economic model where 100% of NFT sales revenue is deposited into liquid staking protocols (JitoSOL, mSOL). The yield automatically buys back and burns our $GCH token, creating a sustainable, deflationary economy backed by real yield rather than speculation. Additional features include NFT lending, perpetual prediction markets via Drift Protocol, and a penalty betting minigame.

**Technology:** Built on Solana (Rust/Anchor smart contracts) with DeFi integrations including Drift Protocol (derivatives), Jito & Marinade (liquid staking), Pyth Network (oracles), and Metaplex Bubblegum (compressed NFTs). Web application in React/TypeScript on Vercel. AI infrastructure using Hermes Agent orchestration for 24/7 autonomous content generation and operations.

**Stage:** Pre-revenue, pre-seed. Devnet MVP live at play.goalworld.fun with wallet connection, fixture-based betting, and claim/refund flows. Mainnet launch targeted Q3 2026, aligned with the World Cup 2026 tournament.

**Team:** Nico (CEO/Founder) — blockchain architect and product strategist. Lucas (Lead Developer) — full-stack Solana engineer. Supported by Hermes AI agents for autonomous operations.

**Market Opportunity:** The 2026 FIFA World Cup is a singular, time-bound event with 5B+ global viewers. GoalWorld is positioned to capture this audience with a product that merges sports passion, DeFi economics, and AI-driven content generation.
